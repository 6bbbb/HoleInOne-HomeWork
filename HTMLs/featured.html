Our Awesome Places

Hotel

Golf Paradise Resort is a 5-star hotel located in the heart of the site, offering luxurious accommodation with direct views of the golf courses. It features luxurious rooms and suites with modern design, a spa and a fitness center equipped with the latest equipment. It also features an outdoor swimming pool with a water bar, and multiple restaurants serving international and local dishes. The hotel also offers conference and banquet halls equipped for business events or special occasions, and a free shuttle service within the site.

Clubs

Golf Masters Club is a private club for distinguished members, offering luxurious services and integrated facilities. It has a private lounge for members overlooking the course, and a fine restaurant serving international dishes. It also provides private lounges and swimming pools, and holds social events and parties for members. Family Golf Club is a club designed to welcome families, where everyone can enjoy their time. It has a children's play area, and golf lessons for children and teenagers. It also holds weekly family events, and provides cafes and family restaurants.
